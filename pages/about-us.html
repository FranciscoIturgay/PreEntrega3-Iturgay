About Us

Does Santa Julia Exist?

Who is Santa Julia?” People who visit our winery often ask this question. Well, I’m Julia. My name is on one of the very first wines made by my family. Santa Julia and I grew up together. We belong to the same family. We share the same values. We enjoy being part of joyous occasions, get-togethers, and large tables. Santa Julia and I are always looking for quality in what we do, we have a bond with the land that watched us grow, and we believe in the importance of taking care of it. We are committed to being useful to our community and its people.”
